Principal Investigator

JC

Dr Joe Chen

Lecturer · Department of Physics

Dr Joe Chen (Chun-Wei Chen) leads the Fibre Photonics Lab at the University of Bath. He earned his PhD in Electrical Engineering from Penn State University and held postdoctoral positions at Yale (with Prof. Hui Cao) and Stanford's Ginzton Laboratory (with Prof. Michel Digonnet). His work spans heat-free fibre lasers, the control of nonlinearities in multimode fibres, and chiral liquid-crystalline photonic crystals, with 45+ peer-reviewed publications.

Postdoctoral Researchers

TF

Dr Ting-Mao Feng

Visiting Postdoctoral Researcher

Ting-Mao Feng earned his PhD in Photonics from National Sun Yat-sen University, Taiwan. His research centres on reconfigurable chiral and soft photonic crystals based on liquid crystals, including tunable electro-optic and polarisation-control devices. His work has appeared in journals such as Nature Communications, Nature Materials, ACS Nano, and Applied Physics Reviews.
