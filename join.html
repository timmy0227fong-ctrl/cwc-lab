Fully Funded PhD Position — September 2026 Entry

A fully funded PhD position is available in the lab on "high-power fibre lasers with intelligent wavefront control", co-supervised by Prof. William Wadsworth.

The position is open to both home and international students. Informal enquiries are welcome — please contact Dr Chun-Wei (Joe) Chen before applying. Applications are made via FindAPhD. Deadline: 18 January 2026.

Postdocs
