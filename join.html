Join Us

We welcome people passionate about cognitive science and computational modelling.

PhD Students

We regularly recruit PhD students with backgrounds in psychology, computer science, or related fields. Please get in touch before applying to discuss research directions and funding opportunities.

Postdocs

Where suitable research funding or fellowship opportunities arise, we are happy to support strong postdoctoral applications.

MSc / Undergraduate Projects

Students at our university interested in project work are welcome to contact us at the start of each term.

If you are interested, please send your CV and a brief statement of research interests to cwc-lab@examplebridge.ac.uk.
