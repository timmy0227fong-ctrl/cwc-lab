About the Lab

University of Examplebridge · School of Psychology

The CWC Lab, led by Prof. Eleanor Hartwell, is based in the School of Psychology at the University of Examplebridge. Our research spans cognitive psychology, computational modelling, and artificial intelligence. We aim to understand the mechanisms underlying human decision-making and emotion regulation, and to develop digital tools that promote mental wellbeing. The lab is run by an interdisciplinary team of postdocs, PhD students, and MSc students.

Research Areas

Three themes we currently focus on

Cognitive Modelling

Using Bayesian and neural network models to simulate human decision-making under uncertainty.

Emotion & Wellbeing

Investigating the neural and behavioural mechanisms of emotion regulation, and designing digital interventions.

Human–AI Interaction

Studying trust, learning, and cognitive load when people interact with AI systems.
